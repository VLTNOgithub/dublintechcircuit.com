What is Dublin Tech Circuit?

"Just like every school in Ireland has a GAA team, it's time for schools to have a tech team."

Dublin Tech Circuit is Ireland's first every post-primary student-led community of technology enthusiasts that fosters peer-to-peer
learning through intra-school collaboration and inter-school competition, covering all things related to technology.
